GELECTRIC MEDICAL

Our company specializes in an innovative electrical adhesive gel patch. This reversibly adhesive patch adheres securely to tissue surfaces, allowing targeted treatment where standard sutures fail. Through the phenomena of electroadhesion, our adhesive gel patch has the potential to revolutionize tissue repairs.

TRANSFORMING SURGICAL TECHNIQUES

Gelectric Medical's approach is simple yet revolutionary. Our adhesive gel patch provides secure adhesion to tissue surfaces via application of an electric field, enabling targeted treatment in areas where traditional sutures may not suffice. The adhesive properties can be reversed by changing the polarity of the electrical field.
